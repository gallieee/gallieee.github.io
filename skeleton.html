Who Am I?

Hi! My name is Allie Carlson and I am a student at UW Madison studying Computer Science. I also am a cake connoisseur with a passion for encouraging women and girls in STEM. In my free time, I like to bake as well as play and create video games. In high school I was a captain of the robotics team. Our team won a regional competition and went to the worlds level of play. Now, I am involved with UW Madison's Game Development Club and am working as both a writer and a programmer on a project called garage sale. I also enjoy conducting research on video games and women in STEM.
